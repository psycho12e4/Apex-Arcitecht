Mission Control

Your comprehensive guide to mastering Apex Architect. Learn how to design, save, and export world-class circuits.

01

Authentication & Setup

Welcome to Apex Architect! Before designing your circuits, you'll need to create an account.

  • Click on Log In from the main dashboard.
  • Sign up with your email and a secure password.
  • Important: You must verify your email address. Check your inbox for a verification link from our system.
  • Once verified, your circuits will be securely synced to the cloud, allowing access from anywhere.
02

Creating a New Circuit

Start with a blank canvas to construct your masterpiece.

  • On the Workspace Dashboard, click the bold New Circuit button.
  • You'll be transported to the Circuit Designer interface.
  • Use the mouse to click and place track nodes on the map.
  • The system will automatically connect your nodes, forming the track layout.
03

Exporting Your Data (JSON & PNG)

Once your circuit is finalized, you can export it for external tools, simulators, or presentations.

  • Export as JSON: In the designer, look for the Export JSON option. This will download a structured file containing all your track's nodes and coordinate data, perfect for importing into a game engine or simulation software.
  • Export as PNG: Click the Export Image button to take a high-resolution snapshot of your track layout. This transparent image can be used for presentations, documents, or sharing with the community.
  • All tracks are continuously synced to your account, so you can always return later to make edits or re-export.
